Break Keyboards With Friends

2 players battle it out to conquer the keyboard

DO NOT PLAY THIS ALONE, CLOSE THE TAB, GET A FRIEND

When it's your turn, hit as many keys as possible to conquer them.

Steal your opponents territory, and avoid helping him when it's his turn (there's no pause between turns...)

The game doesn't enforce this but YOU LOSE if you stop the game's flow - hit the windows key, backspace, F5, Ctrl-F, Ctrl-R, Ctrl-U, Ctrl-O, Ctrl-S, etc.
